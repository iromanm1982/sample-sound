Privacy Policy

App: AudioGroups
Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

AudioGroups is a local soundboard application. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. No data ever leaves your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

AudioGroups does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports

No Accounts, No Ads, No Purchases

AudioGroups does not require an account. It contains no advertisements and no in-app purchases. It does not connect to any server or external service.

Device Permission: Read Audio Files

The app requests the READ_MEDIA_AUDIO permission (or READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE on Android 12 and below). This permission is used solely to let you browse and select audio files already stored on your device. The app never reads, copies, or uploads those files anywhere — they are only played back locally using the device's audio system.

Local Storage

AudioGroups stores your groups and button configuration in a local database on your device (Android Room). This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

AudioGroups does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR). The app is safe for all ages.

Your Rights (GDPR)

As the app does not collect or process any personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any questions, you may contact us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change in the future, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date will be revised. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
